A Brief History of Time

A Brief History of Time

I always wanted to be an olympian be it in cycling, triathlon or athletics thats what I wanted. Sadly in 2018 I realised this wasn't probably going to happen and ended up wanting to commit time to Maths and Comp Sci.

2000

Being Born

Obviously I do not have the most vivid memories from this point in time, however was quite a pivotal point in my life.

2011

Started Athletics

After years of doing Triathlon and hating it, me and my family quit and started to pursue athletics. It was here where I found much greater success in sport and where I feel most relaxed.

2014

Started Learning to Code

Whilst being forced to learn Python at the age of 14 is not everyones idea of fun, it really resonated with me. I constantly found myself more and more interested with what you could do with this beautiful scripting language. Python will always be my favourite.

2019

Started University

I finished my secondary school career achieveing A*A*AA at A-Level and ended up where I wanted to be at Manchester University. I could not have imagined myself anywhere else because of how much I adore the people I live with.

2019
